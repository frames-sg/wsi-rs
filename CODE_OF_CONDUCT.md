# Code of Conduct

This project is committed to a respectful, harassment-free contribution
environment.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct, constructive, and respectful.
- Assume good faith while still asking for evidence when technical claims
  affect security, correctness, or maintainability.
- Keep discussion focused on the work and its impact on users.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, abuse, or discriminatory language.
- Public disclosure of private security reports before coordinated disclosure.
- Personal attacks, repeated disruption, or bad-faith participation.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns or private security issues to the maintainers by email.
Maintainers will review reports promptly, keep sensitive details limited to
people who need them, and take proportionate action when needed.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may warn, moderate, temporarily restrict, or permanently remove
participants from project spaces for unacceptable behavior.
